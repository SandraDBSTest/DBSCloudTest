How to get here

From Shannon Airport:

  • 40 miles/80 km from Shannon Airport.

From Dublin:

  • By Train: IrishRail
  • By Bus: Bus Éireann from Busáras
  • By Car: Approx. 2 hour drive from Dublin. Leave Dublin Continue along: N81 / Cnoc Chorcaí / Cork Hill Turn left : N4 Continue until Kilcock exit to M4, M6, N6 Cappataggle, 2nd exit N6 towards Galway. Turn right R336, and continue along Williamsgate Street until Best View Street to the right.
